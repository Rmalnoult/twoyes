## Full Description (both stores)

Finding the perfect baby name is one of the biggest decisions you'll make as parents — and it's even harder when two people need to agree! TwoYes makes it fun.

**How it works:**
Swipe right on names you love, left on ones you don't. When you and your partner both swipe right on the same name — it's a match!

**Key features:**
- Swipe through thousands of curated baby names
- Partner matching: find names you both love
- AI-powered recommendations based on your taste
- Browse by country: US, France, UK, Germany, Spain, Italy
- Filter by gender, origin, popularity, and more
- Detailed name profiles: meaning, origin, pronunciation, famous namesakes
- Beautiful, playful design made for parents-to-be

**Names from around the world:**
Our database includes official name data from 6 countries — SSA (US), INSEE (France), ONS (UK), Cologne Open Data (Germany), INE (Spain), and ISTAT (Italy). Every name is enriched with AI-generated meanings, cultural context, and style tags.

**Free to use. Made with love for parents-to-be.**
